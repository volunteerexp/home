Beautiful City Called Vienna

From September 29, 2023, to June 30, 2024, I volunteered at the Aftercare Club in Vienna through the European Solidarity Corps. My role involved supporting 14 kids with their homework and activities, ensuring they felt motivated and supported. We engaged in arts, crafts, games, and sports, and I shared stories and traditions from my home country, which the kids enjoyed. Living in Vienna was fantastic; I shared a flat with volunteers from Belgium and Spain, and we bonded over cooking, exploring the city, and learning about each other’s cultures. A highlight was meeting volunteers from around the world and making new friendships. I also improved my German skills, which was a personal goal. Overall, the experience was enriching, offering daily learning, new experiences, and meaningful connections.
